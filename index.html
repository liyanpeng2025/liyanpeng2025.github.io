Yanpeng Li

 

Associate Researcher of Mathematics and Statistics,
School of Mathematics, Harbin Institute of Technology

I received my PhD in Statistics from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2023 under the supervision of Prof. Boping Tian and Prof. Wang Zhou. I am also a MYSP postdoctoral fellow at University of Macao under the supervision of Prof. Zhi Liu in 2025.08-2027.07).

I am broadly interested in the mathematics of data science, including random matrix theory, high dimensional statistics, learning theory, and information theory. Recently I am learning the theory of 6-vertex model as well as its relation to random matrix theory.
